The Sun on my Fridge

By Emiliano Elizalde

This story is about finding inspiration. The protagonist is going thruogh art block but finds something that sucks him in and inspires him to make art again. I made this because I felt inspired by a painting of a furnace. To me, the message is that inspiration can come in the weirdest ways, you just have to be ready to channel it into good work.
